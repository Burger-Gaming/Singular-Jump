-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
INTRODUCTION
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Welcome to Singular Jump!
This was just a small game I decided to make in about 3 days and about an hour of development.
(this is false information i took around 5 months of a break when i reached the third day mark)

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
CONTROLS (Arrow Keys)
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Left & Right - Move
Up - Jump

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
GAMEPLAY
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The objective of the game is to not fall into the pot! There are a few difficulties implemented, but more will (probably) be planned.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
ADDITIONAL GAME SETTINGS
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Flip X - Flips the camera horizontally.
Flip Y - Flips the camera vertically.
Void Mode - Basically a blindfolded mode.

You can access these by pressing G in the difficulty menu.
